Terms of Use

At Tires, we are committed to ensuring the quality of the products we offer, and we make it easy for our customers to access warranty services provided by authorized suppliers or manufacturers. Here are the details of our warranty policy:

1- Warranty Comprehensiveness

The warranty covers only manufacturing defects in tires, such as:
  • Poor manufacturing.
  • Defects in materials or internal structure.
  • Separation of layers or tire inflation without an external cause.
  • The warranty period varies depending on the type of tire and manufacturer, and typically ranges from 6 months to 1 year, or depending on the local dealer's policy.

    • The warranty is provided by the authorized dealer or supplier, not directly from the platform. Unless otherwise stated on the product page, the following conditions must be met: Unless otherwise stated on the product page, the following conditions must be met: ... (excess or under-inflated).

  • Unprofessional installation.

    • 3- Warranty Claim Procedures

    If you encounter a problem with your frame during the warranty period, please follow these steps:

    • 1. Contact us via customer service (or directly with the supplier if named on the invoice).
    • 2. Send clear photos of the damaged frame.
    • 3. Indicate the order number, purchase date, and customer location.
    • 4. You will be directed to the nearest authorized inspection center affiliated with the supplier or manufacturer.
    • After the inspection, one of the following options will be taken based on the technician's report:
    • Replace the tire with a new one (depending on the extent of the damage).
    • Partial compensation (a percentage of the price based on the tire's age).
    • Reject the warranty if the cause is found to be outside the coverage area.
